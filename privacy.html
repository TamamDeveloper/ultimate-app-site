Privacy Policy

Ultimate · Last updated: December 15, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Ultimate ("the App") handles your information. "My Products" is developed and maintained by the developer of My Products ("we", "us", "our").

We believe privacy should be the default, not an option. Ultimate is designed so that your data never leaves your device.

1. Information We Collect and Use

The App requests access to certain features and data on your device to provide its core functionality. Below is a summary of the types of data accessed and their purpose:

  • Camera: Used to take photos of documents. The App accesses your camera only with your explicit permission. Images are processed locally and are not transmitted to our servers or third parties.
  • Photo Library: Used to upload photos of documents. The App accesses your photo library only with your explicit permission. Images are processed locally and are not transmitted to our servers or third parties.

2. Data Storage and Sharing

  • No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted to our servers by default.
  • Any information accessed or created within the App (such as photos or scanned documents) remains on your device unless you choose to share it yourself.
  • The App does not use analytics or advertising SDKs, and we do not track your activity.

3. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18.

4. International Use

The App is available worldwide. Regardless of your location, your data is not collected or transferred outside your device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
