PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: December 15, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Ultimate ("the App") handles your information. "My Products" is developed and maintained by the developer of My Products ("we", "us", "our").

We believe privacy should be the default, not a setting. Ultimate is designed so that your data never leaves your device — period.

🎾 Short version: The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data anywhere. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect and Use

The App requests access to certain features on your device to provide its core functionality. Below is a full summary:

  • Camera: Used to photograph documents and tournament participants. Accessed only with your explicit permission. Images are processed locally and never transmitted to any server or third party.
  • Photo Library: Used to upload photos (documents, group tournament shots). Accessed only with your explicit permission. Images are processed locally and never transmitted to any server or third party.

2. Data Storage and Sharing

  • No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted to our servers by default.
  • Any information created within the App (player names, scores, brackets, photos) remains on your device unless you choose to share it yourself.
  • The App does not use analytics or advertising SDKs. We do not track your activity in any way.

3. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18.

4. International Use

The App is available worldwide. Regardless of your location, your data is not collected or transferred outside your device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated above. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.
